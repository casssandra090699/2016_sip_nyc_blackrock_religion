ISLAM

Islam originated in Saudi Arabia during the 7th century. Islam is established on the teachings of Muhammad, which can be found in the Qurâ€™an, the sacred text of Islam. The principal belief of Islam is that Allah is the one and true God as well as the creator of the world. Islam is divided into 2 denominations, Sunni and Shia. both of which have different views regarding the leadership of the Muslim nation. Islam is practiced by 1.6 billion people.
